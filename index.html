Country Jai · Artist profile

A UK artist with a country heart – balancing everyday life, big dreams, and songs that sound just as at home in a small bar as they do on a festival stage.

Country Jai weaves real stories into modern country tracks – the kind you belt out on a long drive or keep on repeat when you need a lift. Blending classic influences with a chart-ready sound, the music draws on family, friends, football, and the highs and lows of life in the UK.

From heartfelt ballads to crowd-ready anthems, every song is built around one idea: if it matters to real people, it belongs in a song.
